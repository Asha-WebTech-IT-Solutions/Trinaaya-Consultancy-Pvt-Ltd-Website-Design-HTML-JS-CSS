About Trinaaya

Understanding the vision behind India's trusted financial consultancy.

The Meaning Behind Trinaaya

The name "Trinaaya" is inspired by the Sanskrit concept of the "third eye" — representing clarity, deeper insight, and the ability to see what others miss.

We bring this philosophy to financial consultancy. Where others see complexity, we see structure. Where others see obstacles, we see opportunities. Our focus is on providing structured financial clarity that empowers our clients to make informed decisions.

Founded by Kunal Handa, who brings over 16 years of deep banking expertise, Trinaaya was built on the belief that every individual and business deserves access to quality financial advisory — not just the privileged few.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To democratize access to financial advisory services. We believe that smart financial guidance should be available to everyone — from first-time homebuyers to established businesses seeking growth capital. Our mission is to bridge the gap between aspiration and achievement through expert financial consultation.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted financial advisory platform. We envision a future where every financial decision is backed by expert guidance, every funding need is met with the right solution, and every client relationship is built on transparency, trust, and long-term value creation.
